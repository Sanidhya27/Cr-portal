About Us

Why Mood Indigo?

Mood Indigo, IIT Bombay is Asia's largest college cultural festival. It annually entertains a footfall of more than 1,39,000 students from around 1650 colleges span across India. Over the years. Mood Indigo has played host to lots of artists, celebrities and events spanning 20 genres.

Mood Indigo's CR system plays an integral part in its growth, serving as a major platform for connecting Mood Indigo with college students across the country as well as ideating different events and artist lineups. Stand out from the throngs of people in your college by associating with us as our College Representative!

Responsibilities as a CR

Represent Mood Indigo in your college

Be the face of Mood Indigo by publicizing our events in your college and on social media.

Ideation

Give suggestions for new events, genres and artists you want to see in MI, besides brainstorming for venues for pre-MI events in your city.

Coordination and Public Relations

Coordinating with the Mood Indigo team and helping organise Multicity events in your city along with managing public relations from Mood Indigo’s end with various cultural clubs and bodies in your college.
